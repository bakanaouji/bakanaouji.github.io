I study bandits and online learning, which address the exploration–exploitation trade-off in sequential decision making. This includes efficient exploration in high-dimensional and non-stationary environments, Bayesian and hyperparameter optimization, and applications to online advertising and bidding.

## Key questions
- How can we make exploration efficient in high-dimensional, non-stationary settings?
- How can we improve Bayesian optimization under a limited budget?
- How can online learning be applied to real-world bidding and delivery?
